About Us
🌿

Our Beginning

From a modest beginning in Salalah, Dallah Fresh started as a local catering and fresh-produce supplier, serving nearby communities and hospitality businesses through a single outlet and a dedicated delivery fleet.

Our Growth

What began as a regional operation soon expanded into hospitality provisioning, institutional supply, and large-scale nationwide distribution.

Our Evolution

Over the years, Dallah Fresh evolved into a fully integrated fresh-produce company, strengthening its import network, building trusted partnerships with Omani farms, and introducing modern retail and online sales channels.
