Global Sourcing

Premium Produce From Around the World

Dallah Fresh combines international sourcing with established partnerships across Omani farms to maintain year-round availability and seasonal flexibility. The company works closely with local growers to support crop planning, consistent volumes and quality alignment, reinforcing its contribution to domestic agriculture.

Complementing domestic supply, Dallah Fresh maintains active procurement channels across key producing regions. This geographically diversified network strengthens supply continuity, mitigates seasonal risk and enables responsive sourcing across a wide product portfolio.

  • 10+ source countries across 4 continents
  • Direct farm partnerships for quality control
  • Seasonal & year-round availability
  • Full traceability from field to shelf
Local Partnerships

Supporting Oman's Local Farmers

We believe in strengthening local agriculture. Our partnerships with Omani farmers ensure fresh, locally-grown produce reaches our clients while supporting the national agricultural economy.

  • Direct partnerships with Omani farms
  • Supporting local agricultural development
  • Same-day harvest-to-delivery for select items
  • Seasonal Omani specialties and organic options
