Cold Storage

Multi-Zone Temperature Control

Our warehouses feature advanced multi-zone temperature control systems, maintaining different temperature ranges for different types of produce:

  • Chilled Zone (2-8°C): Fresh fruits, leafy vegetables
  • Cool Zone (8-14°C): Root vegetables, citrus fruits
  • Ambient Zone (14-20°C): Tropical fruits, onions, potatoes

All zones are equipped with real-time temperature monitoring, humidity control, and automated alert systems to ensure optimal storage conditions 24/7.
